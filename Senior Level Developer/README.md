## 💡 How to use this kit
- **Depth over Breadth**: At the senior level, interviewers care about the trade-offs. Don't just give the "correct" answer; explain the scenarios where it might fail.
- **Real-world Examples**: Try to map these answers to your own past experiences. Interviewers love hearing: *"In my previous project at X, we faced this exact issue and we solved it by..."*
- **The "Business Why"**: Always relate technical decisions back to business value (Cost, Speed, Reliability).

Good luck with your senior engineering interviews!
